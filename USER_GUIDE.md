## Mood Tracking

### Why Track Mood?

Mood tracking helps you:
- Identify patterns and triggers
- Recognize when you need support
- Celebrate positive trends
- Provide data for mental health conversations

### How to Log Mood

**Method 1: Dashboard Quick Add**
1. Click **+ Mood** button on dashboard
2. Select mood score (1-10 scale)
3. Add optional notes (what influenced your mood?)
4. Click **Save**

**Method 2: Mood Entry Page**
1. Navigate to **Mood Tracker** tab
2. Click **New Mood Entry**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Entry Date:** Today (default) or backdate
   - **Mood Score:** 1 (very low) to 10 (excellent)
   - **Notes:** Describe what contributed to this mood
   - **Tags:** Add keywords (anxiety, excited, tired, etc.)
4. Click **Save**

### Mood Scale Guide

- **10 - Euphoric:** Exceptional day, everything feels great
- **8-9 - Happy:** Positive mood, productive, content
- **6-7 - Good:** Feeling okay, some ups and downs
- **4-5 - Neutral:** Neither good nor bad, just existing
- **3 - Low:** Struggling, feeling down
- **1-2 - Very Low:** Significant distress, need support

**Important:** Scores of 1-3 trigger automatic wellness check-in emails with resources.

### Viewing Mood Trends

**Analytics View:**
1. Navigate to **Mood Tracker** ‚Üí **Analytics**
2. View visualizations:
   - Line chart: Mood over time
   - Average by day of week
   - Pattern detection (e.g., "Mondays are tough")

**AI Insights:**
- NeuroThrive uses Claude AI to analyze mood patterns
- Look for "Mood Insights" card on dashboard
- Insights refresh weekly

### Mood History

**Edit Previous Entries:**
1. Find the mood entry in your history
2. Click entry to open

## Therapy Tools

### Available Therapy Modalities

NeuroThrive includes guided exercises for:

#### 1. CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy)
**Purpose:** Challenge and reframe negative thoughts

**When to Use:**
- Experiencing negative self-talk
- Catastrophizing or black-and-white thinking
- Imposter syndrome thoughts

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Therapy Tools** ‚Üí **CBT Exercise**
2. Describe the negative thought
3. AI guides you through:
   - Identifying thought distortions
   - Finding evidence for/against the thought
   - Reframing into balanced perspective
4. Save completed exercise

#### 2. DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy)
**Purpose:** Emotion regulation and distress tolerance

**When to Use:**
- Feeling overwhelmed by emotions
- Need coping strategies
- Interpersonal conflicts

**Exercises:**
- **STOP Skill:** Pause, Take a breath, Observe, Proceed mindfully
- **TIPP Skill:** Temperature, Intense exercise, Paced breathing, Paired muscle relaxation
- **Wise Mind:** Balance emotion and logic

#### 3. Grounding Techniques
**Purpose:** Manage anxiety and panic

**5-4-3-2-1 Technique:**
1. Navigate to **Therapy Tools** ‚Üí **Grounding**
2. Follow prompts to identify:
   - 5 things you see
   - 4 things you can touch
   - 3 things you hear
   - 2 things you smell
   - 1 thing you taste
3. Record how you feel after

#### 4. Mindfulness Meditation
**Purpose:** Present-moment awareness

**Guided Meditations:**
- 5-minute breathing exercise
- Body scan meditation
- Loving-kindness meditation

### Tracking Therapy Progress

**Therapy Completions:**
- All exercises are logged to `Therapy_Step_Completion__c`
- View your therapy history
- Track which techniques work best for you

**Analytics:**
- Mood before vs. after therapy exercise
- Most effective techniques
- Frequency of practice

### Crisis Resources

**If you're in crisis, NeuroThrive provides immediate access to:**
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text 988
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text "HELLO" to 741741
- **SAMHSA Helpline:** 1-800-662-4357
- **Emergency:** 911

**Accessing Resources:**
- Click **Get Help Now** button on dashboard (appears if mood scores are very low)
- Navigate to **Resources** ‚Üí **Crisis Support**
- Check your email for wellness check-in messages

## Privacy & Data

### What Data is Collected?

NeuroThrive stores:
- **Personal Wellness Data:**
  - Mood scores and notes
  - Win entries
  - Gratitude entries
  - Therapy exercise completions
- **Meal Planning Data:**
  - Recipes you view and save
  - Weekly meal plans
  - Shopping lists
- **Career Data:**
  - Job postings you're tracking
  - Interview prep responses

### Who Can See My Data?

**Your Data is Private:**
- Only YOU can see your personal wellness data
- System administrators CANNOT access individual mood entries, wins, or gratitude journals
- Data is isolated by user (record-level security)

**Shared Data:**
- Recipe library is shared across all users
- Job posting templates may be shared (if configured)

### Data Retention

**How Long is Data Kept:**
- Mood entries: 2 years
- Win entries: Indefinitely (your achievement record!)
- Gratitude entries: Indefinitely
- Therapy completions: Indefinitely (for tracking progress)
- Meal plans: 1 year
- Shopping lists: 6 months after target date

**Deleting Your Data:**
1. Navigate to object tab (Mood Tracker, Wins, etc.)
2. Select records to delete
3. Click **Delete**
4. Confirm deletion

**Complete Data Deletion:**
- Contact your Salesforce administrator

- All records will be permanently removed within 30 days

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Issue: Dashboard Not Loading

**Symptoms:** Spinning wheel, blank screen

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache:
   - Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear Data
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
4. Check browser console for errors (F12 ‚Üí Console tab)
5. Contact administrator if issue persists

#### Issue: "You don't have access to this record"

**Symptoms:** Error message when trying to view mood/win/gratitude entry

**Cause:** Record created by different user or sharing settings changed

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you're viewing YOUR records (filter by "My Records")
2. Check with administrator about sharing settings
3. If you need access to deleted records, ask admin to restore from Recycle Bin

#### Issue: AI Features Not Working

**Symptoms:** "Error calling Claude API" or blank AI responses

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify API key is configured (admin task):
   - Setup ‚Üí Custom Metadata Types ‚Üí Claude API Settings
   - Ensure API key is valid and not expired
3. Check Salesforce remote site settings:
   - Setup ‚Üí Security ‚Üí Remote Site Settings
   - Ensure `https://api.anthropic.com` is allowed
4. Review debug logs for specific error messages
5. Contact Anthropic support if API quota exceeded

#### Issue: Emails Not Sending

**Symptoms:** Missing weekly summaries or daily reminders

**Solutions:**
1. Check email address in user profile (Settings ‚Üí My Profile)
2. Verify email preferences:
   - Settings ‚Üí Email Preferences
   - Ensure summaries are enabled
3. Check spam/junk folder
4. Whitelist sender: noreply@salesforce.com
5. Administrator checks:
   - Setup ‚Üí Email Deliverability ‚Üí "All Email"
   - Setup ‚Üí Flows ‚Üí Verify flows are Active
   - Setup ‚Üí Scheduled Jobs ‚Üí Verify schedulers are running

#### Issue: Meal Plan Calendar Not Displaying

**Symptoms:** Blank calendar or "Component Error"

**Solutions:**
1. Verify Lightning Web Component is deployed:
   - Setup ‚Üí Lightning Components ‚Üí Search "mealPlanCalendar"
2. Check browser console for JavaScript errors (F12)
3. Ensure Weekly Meal Plan has meals added
4. Try creating a new meal plan
5. Contact administrator to check component visibility settings

#### Issue: Shopping List Not Generating from Meal Plan

**Symptoms:** "Generate Shopping List" button does nothing or shows error

**Solutions:**
1. Verify meal plan has recipes with ingredients
2. Check that recipes have ingredient lists populated
3. Review debug logs for errors:
   - Developer Console ‚Üí Logs
   - Look for "ShoppingListController" errors
4. Manually create shopping list as workaround
5. Report issue to administrator with meal plan ID

#### Issue: Interview Prep Not Generating Questions

**Symptoms:** Blank questions or "Unable to generate questions"

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure job posting has description filled in
2. Provide more detail in "Required Skills" field
3. Check Claude API connectivity (see AI Features issue above)
4. Manually add practice questions as workaround
5. Review job description for special characters that may break parsing

## Glossary

**Terms Used in NeuroThrive:**

- **Daily Routine:** Container record for a day's wellness activities
- **Mood Entry:** Single mood score with notes for a specific time
- **Win Entry:** Achievement or positive event logged
- **Gratitude Entry:** Reflection on something you're thankful for
- **Therapy Step Completion:** Record of completing a therapy exercise
- **Weekly Meal Plan:** 7-day meal planning container
- **Meal:** Individual breakfast/lunch/dinner within meal plan
- **Recipe:** Template with ingredients and instructions
- **Shopping List:** Collection of items to purchase
- **Job Posting:** Job you're tracking/applying for
- **STAR Method:** Situation, Task, Action, Result (interview framework)

**Salesforce Terms:**

- **Dashboard:** Visual summary page
- **Record:** Single database entry (like a row in a spreadsheet)
- **Object:** Type of data (like a table in a database)
- **Field:** Individual data point (like a column in a spreadsheet)
- **App Launcher:** Grid icon to switch between Salesforce apps
- **Lightning Experience:** Modern Salesforce interface
